About

✏️ UI Design

I create contemporary and unique designs for websites and mobile apps. I have a deep understanding of interaction design principles, typography, color theory, layout, icons, styleguides and much more.

🧠 UX Design

I love building interactive wireframes and prototypes to bring ideas to life. My focus is on creating user-friendly visuals that showcase concepts in a tangible way, guiding the path from initial ideas to practical mockups.

💼 Long professional experience

Over 20 years of professional experience in the agency sector with a wide variety of clients give me a wealth of experience that I can use to provide my clients with holistic advice.

👩‍💻 Figma

Figma skills on expert level for vivid prototypes, wireframes and design files.

(Sure, Adobe's there too, but seriously, who's still riding that software train like it's the last Uber home after a party?)

🤓 Design Systems

I have a keen emphasis on crafting scalable design systems and intelligent component libraries, utilizing Figma as my primary tool. This approach ensures designs that seamlessly adapt to evolving project requirements.

💛 Soft skills

Proficient in quickly understanding concepts and maintaining a keen focus on high-quality outcomes. I possess strong organizational and planning abilities, adept at solving problems with a human-centered approach and empathy. I am an effective communicator with a knack for clear and concise interaction.

I am an User Interface Designer with more than 20 years of professional experience in the digital agency industry as Senior UX/UI Designer and Creative Director. I was responsible for the development of digital products and for leading creative teams. I have helped develop visual digital strategies for many large companies and am very familiar with all aspects of UX and UI design. My activities focused on the conceptual and visual development of complex portals and apps as well as IT ecosystems.

Since 5 years I am more than happy to work as a freelancer. I love creative challenges, technical processes and developing solutions that require all my experience and creativity. 

Through my digital expertise, my design background, my technical understanding and my further education as a coach, I create results of particular value. This starts with the development of the concept, includes the creative design process and is reflected in my very personal advice.
