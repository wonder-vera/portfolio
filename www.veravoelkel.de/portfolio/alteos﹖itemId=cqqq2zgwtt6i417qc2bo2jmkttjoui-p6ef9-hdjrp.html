ALTEOS

Responsive Website

alteos.com

Year

2023/24

The Challenge

To comply with my non-disclosure agreement, I have omitted confidential information in this case study. All information is my own and does not necessarily reflect the views of Alteos.

Alteos enables companies to integrate insurance seamlessly into their offering. Thanks to a wide range of interfaces, insurance solutions can be quickly and easily integrated into companies' product landscapes. The Alteos tech platform automates all administrative processes from conclusion to claims settlement.

In 2023, Alteos decided to redesign its website to make it clearer for customers and to give the design a fresh and modern look.

I was part of the project of building the Alteos homepage to give users a better experience whether chilling in a chair at home or heading to work.

Our High Level Goals

  • Drawing users attention to the product informations to persuade them

  • Get in contact with the Alteos Team (via phonecall or email)

  • Making the site so flexible so that new products and business areas can be easily added to the site


My Role

As a UX/UI designer, I started first with the strategic placement and arrangement of information, modules and content, information architecture, user flow and wireframes. Then I developed the design according to the new brand specifications. In doing so I collaborated closely with the Alteos product and marketing team.

Main Tasks

  1. Renewal of the page structure

  2. Creation of conceptual wireframes

  3. Design Execution and validation

  4. Building the component library in Figma

  5. Creating the style guide.


Tools used

Figma, Miro
