Living in Berlin, I'm an User Interface Designer with over 20 years of experience in the design and concept of digital products such as websites and mobile apps. I help clients communicate and solve problems through creative visual solutions.

My expertise lies in Figma, where I produce seamless digital experiences that marry functionality, ensuring users feel right at home.
